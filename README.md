# Project-in-Power-BI Laptop_Sales
Project Overview:
Designed and developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze laptop sales data across multiple dimensions such as state, region, city, brand, processor type, and time period. The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

Key Metrics Analyzed:

Total Sales Amount: 493M

Total Profit: 65M

Total Quantity Sold: 5K units

Total Unit Price Value: 121M

Dashboard Features & Analysis:

Analyzed profit by state and brand (Acer, Apple, ASUS, Dell) to identify high-performing regions

Visualized monthly and quarterly sales trends to track performance over time

Evaluated unit price distribution by city to compare regional pricing patterns

Analyzed sales quantity by processor type (i3, i5, i7, M2, Ryzen 5, Ryzen 7) to understand customer preferences

Implemented geographical analysis using map visuals for region-wise insights

Interactivity & Usability:

Dynamic slicers for State, Region, Order Quarter, and Month

Drill-down capabilities for deeper analysis

Clean and intuitive dashboard layout for business users

Tools & Technologies Used:

Power BI

Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)

Excel (Data Source)

Business Impact:

Helped identify high-profit states and brands

Enabled comparison of sales trends across regions and time periods

Supported better sales planning and product strategy decisions

Key Skills Demonstrated:
Power BI • Data Visualization • DAX • Data Modeling • Business Intelligence • Analytical Thinking
